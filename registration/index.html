Registration Process

Registration for lodging and meals is due October 1. After that date, you can still register to attend the conference (until October 10), but on-site accommodations may no longer be available. A single Housing/Meals fee covers both accommodations and meals for the duration of the conference. A separate (and smaller) Conference Registration fee covers attendance at the scientific sessions.

Conference Registration

Conference Registration Deadline: Oct 10, 2023

There are two steps to register for the conference:

  1. Pay the registration fee at the appropriate link, making note of your transaction number:
    • $50 for students and postdocs
    • $65 for faculty and professionals
  2. Fill out and submit the conference registration form, being sure to include the transaction number from your payment.

Please note that conference registration does not reserve lodging or arrange for meals. To reserve a room and register for meals, follow the instructions below.

Registration for Housing and Meals

Housing/Meals Registration Deadline: Oct 1, 2023

To register for meals and housing complete the housing registration form and send the completed form and payment to Kristina Hale (khale@dnr.in.gov) or Samara Sheetz (ssheetz@dnr.in.gov) at Turkey Run Inn by October 1, 2023.

All housing registration must be made directly with Turkey Run Inn.

Housing Registration Options:

  • 1 person, 1 bed per room $450
  • 2 person, 2 beds per room $350 per person
